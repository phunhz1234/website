Reservation

Chwes restaurant is the perfect restaurant to host for reservations for many types of ocations such as birthday party, anniversary and many more. When you book a reservation with us, you will be guaranteed a table upon your arrival based on the reservations made in advance. Reservations are to be made atleats 3 days in advance. By having a reservation you won't have to worry about the seating position as there will be a reserved area allocated based on the number of guest stated when you booked the reservation. Although reservations were made for a special occations, strictly no outside food or drinks are allowed in the restaurant. Due to the ongoing pandemic all customers have to be wearing a mask at all times. without a mask you are not allowed to enter even if reservations were made due to the safety of our customers.
